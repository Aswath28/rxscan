## Why Rx Scan?

Three things break in Indian healthcare:

1. You can't read your doctor's handwriting
2. You don't know what the medicines actually do
3. You overpay for branded medicines when CDSCO-certified generics exist

Rx Scan fixes all three. In one scan.

💰 **Save 50–90% on every prescription** — Branded *Augmentin 625* costs ₹250. The generic equivalent (Amoxicillin + Clavulanic Acid) at Jan Aushadhi: ₹45. Same molecule, same effect, 82% off.

🩺 **Plain-language explanations** — Stop Googling drug names. Get clear answers on what each medicine does, how to take it, and what side effects to watch for.

⚠️ **Drug-interaction safety checks** — Every scan runs cross-medication checks. Catches problems your doctor might not have flagged.

🔒 **Privacy by design** — No signup. No accounts. No data stored. Your prescription stays in your session and disappears when you close the tab.

🇮🇳 **Built for India first** — Indian prescriber shorthand, brand-to-generic mapping (Augmentin → Amoxicillin), and CDSCO-certified generics from Jan Aushadhi.

## How it works

**1. Snap** — Take a photo of your handwritten prescription. Works with any doctor's handwriting.

**2. Read** — AI identifies every medicine, dosage, and frequency — even from messy handwriting.

**3. Save** — See generic alternatives with prices. Know exactly how much you can save.

## Privacy & data handling

This is the part that matters most.

- **No signup, no accounts** — There's nothing to sign up for. Just scan and use.
- **No data stored** — Your prescription lives in your session and is gone when you close the tab.
- **No analytics SDKs** — No tracking, no fingerprinting, no third-party SDKs phoning home.

The cost of "free" cloud health apps is usually your data. Rx Scan refuses that trade.
